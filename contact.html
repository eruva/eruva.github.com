Ask yourself a few questions

The more you know about your product, the faster it is to get started. Here's a few questions you might want to ask yourself about your project:

  • In 50 words or less, describe your product. What's the "elevator pitch"?
  • What's your budget?
  • Do you have any deadlines?
  • Who are the users of your product? It's always good to have an "archetype" in mind before beginning.
  • Do you plan to make money from this web app? If so, how?
